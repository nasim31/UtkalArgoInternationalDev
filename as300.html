Heavy-Duty Hydraulic Breaker AS 300

The Heavy-Duty Hydraulic Breaker is a versatile and robust construction tool engineered to deliver outstanding performance in breaking and demolishing tough materials. Designed for use with hydraulic excavators, backhoes, or skid-steer loaders, this hydraulic hammer is a reliable and essential addition to any construction or demolition project.

ABTS Engineering is a leading manufacturer and exporter of Rock Breakers & Rock Breaker Spare Parts in India. We successfully developed this Rock Breaker (also known as Hydraulic Hammer or Hydraulic Breaker) for the construction, Earthmoving & Mining Sector in India.

ABTS Rock Breaker is proudly made in India. Our state-of-the-art factory produces a comprehensive range of breakers to meet the needs of our customers in various fields such as quarrying, mining, demolition, and construction. Our product consistently delivers sustainable productivity and outperforms other breakers in its class.
