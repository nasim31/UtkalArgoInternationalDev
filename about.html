About Utkal Agro Internationaal

Established in 2014, Utkal Agro International has rapidly emerged as a key player in the mining equipment industry in Odisha and the North Eastern States of India. We are dedicated to offering top-notch sales and service solutions.

Our expanding portfolio now includes collaborations with renowned companies like Soosan Heavy Industries (South Korea) for hydraulic rock breakers and Kubota Agriculture India Pvt Ltd for Mini Excavators.

We have also extended our marketing and service network to cover Odisha and the Eastern states, showcasing our commitment to our valued customers. Our team of experienced engineers is at the forefront of incorporating the latest technology and state-of-the-art formats.

We stay close to our customers, analyzing their needs and continuously innovating to provide connected and automated solutions that enhance their performance. Our service solutions ensure peace of mind and help customers maximize uptime while meeting environmental requirements.

At Utkal Agro International, our customer-centric culture is dedicated to providing the best benefits to our clients. Join us in our journey to redefine excellence in mining and construction equipment.
