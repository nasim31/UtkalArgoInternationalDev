Versatile Hydraulic Breaker SB10 Side

The SB10 Side Hydraulic Breaker is a versatile and robust construction tool designed for breaking and demolishing tough materials with exceptional performance. Engineered for compatibility with hydraulic excavators, backhoes, or skid-steer loaders, it's an essential addition to any construction or demolition project.

ABTS Engineering, a leading manufacturer and exporter of Rock Breakers & Rock Breaker Spare Parts in India, proudly presents the SB10 Side Hydraulic Breaker (also known as Hydraulic Hammer). Crafted in India, our state-of-the-art factory produces a comprehensive range of breakers for quarrying, mining, demolition, and construction, consistently delivering superior productivity.

Available for base machines ranging from 0.8 tons to 2.5 tons, SB10 Side Hydraulic Breakers are distributed and supported nationwide through an extensive network of dealers, ensuring top-notch parts and after-sales service.

About Hydraulic Breaker SB10 Side

Elevate your construction and demolition projects with the SB10 Side Hydraulic Breaker – the ultimate solution for efficiency and productivity on the job site. Invest in a tool that delivers unmatched performance and reliability, ensuring your projects are completed efficiently.

Powerful Demolition Capability: With its high-impact percussive force, the SB10 Side Hydraulic Breaker effortlessly shatters concrete, rocks, asphalt, and challenging materials, significantly reducing project timelines.

Built to Last: Crafted with top-quality materials and precision engineering, the SB10 Side Hydraulic Breaker guarantees long-lasting performance even in demanding conditions. Its robust construction can endure heavy use and extreme environments.
