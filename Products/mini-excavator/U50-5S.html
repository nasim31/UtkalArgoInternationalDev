About KUBOTA Mini-Excavator U50-5S

The Kubota U50-5S offers customers versatility never seen before in the mid size 5 ton mini-excavator market. Powered by a reliable Kubota 38.6 HP engine, the Kubota U50-5S features a zero tail swing and well balanced design, combining exceptional power and the versatility to tackle any job and working condition with ease.

With a focus on operator comfort and safety, this excavator offers a spacious and ergonomic cabin, excellent visibility, and intuitive controls. Its durability and reliability, combined with eco-friendly design, make it a responsible choice for both your business and the environment

At Utkalagro Internationaal , we're proud to introduce you to the KUBOTA U50-5S, a dependable partner in progress for all your construction and excavation needs. Contact us today to learn more about how this exceptional machine can enhance your projects.
