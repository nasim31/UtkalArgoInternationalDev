About KUBOTA Mini-Excavator U30-6S

The KUBOTA U30-6S is a class leading, Zero tail swing mini-excavator offering more room than ever before. Powered by a renowned Kubota 27.5 HP Direct Injection Engine, the KUBOTA U30-6S combines superior Performance and Fuel Efficiency with outstanding versatility to tackle a wide variety of jobs and conditions.
